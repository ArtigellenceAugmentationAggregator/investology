§ 06 ❦ Compliance & Discipline ❦ Non-Negotiable

Your name stays clean.
Your license stays safe.

You're a licensed real estate agent operating as a buyer's advocate. Every piece of content, every outbound email, every voice-agent transcript has to sit inside NSW Fair Trading, Spam Act, and (if you touch SMSF specifics) ASIC-adjacent rules. This is the discipline I hold, not you.

Real estate agent license · NSW Fair Trading

All published content carries a "general information only, not personal financial or property advice" disclaimer. Voice Agent never quotes fees, recommends properties, or confirms deals — captures, qualifies, books, hands off to you. Your NSW real estate agent license number appears in every email footer and site page. Any content touching specific suburbs stays factual, not directional.

ASIC-adjacent · SMSF content care

If content touches SMSF specifics (structuring, LRBA, contribution caps), we frame as "educational information only, seek advice from a licensed SMSF adviser" and never step into personal financial advice territory. Buyer's advocate license covers property; ASIC-regulated advice is a separate license we never claim. Referral partnership with a licensed SMSF adviser is a safer content play — we can build that.

Spam Act 2003 · Australian compliance

Every outbound email carries identification, consent basis, working unsubscribe. Warmed email sent only from investologyhq.online — your main info@investology.com.au is never put at risk. Any prospect who opts out is honoured within 24 hours. Penalties for getting this wrong run to six figures a day. We simply never risk it.

Reviews compliance · ACCC guidelines

All reviews harvested are real, verified, unaltered. No incentivised reviews. Testimonial usage follows the client's original consent. Any client featured in a case study signs a written release. Your review reputation is your most valuable asset — we protect it more carefully than any other.

NDA · confidentiality · code ownership

Mutual NDA signed on request before any deep work. Client data (leads, calls, matched properties) is highly sensitive — encrypted at rest, access-controlled, GDPR-adjacent hygiene. You own the code, the prompts, the credentials, the trained models, the content, the reviews library, the podcast episodes. No lock-in. Break clause at 3 months.
